breath+

Lung health monitoring device

It is like heart rate monitor but for your lungs

Device to monitor and manage asthma

Breathcount measures your lung functionality and sends the data to your smarthpone. There it can be viewed, compared and sent to others.

Free of care

Asthma patients dont need to think about recording their condition. It is done for them. They also receive suggestions based on their results.

Parent's dream

Information about a patient's state is always available online. It can be viewed by doctors, parents, anyone patient grants access to.

Designed for users

Device and applications are designed for daily use. We focus on user needs and made it light, small and beautiful.

Current state

We have created a working prototype!

It measures peak flow and sends it to a desktop via USB
